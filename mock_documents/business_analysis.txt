Title: Q2 Market Signal — Executive Summary

Executive Summary:
This mock business analysis outlines market adoption signals, competitive landscape, revenue projections, and adoption risk for Product Y.

Key Points:
- Market size estimated at $1.2B TAM, with serviceable obtainable market ~12% in year 1.
- Early adopter segments: specialty clinics and integrated health systems.
- Competitive pressure from incumbents A and B may compress margins.

Risks:
- Adoption risk driven by reimbursement uncertainty, clinician workflow disruption, and supply cadence.
- Price sensitivity in non-specialty segments may slow growth.

Recommendations:
- Focus pilot programs on specialty clinics, secure a reimbursement pathway, and create educational materials to reduce perceived workflow burden.
